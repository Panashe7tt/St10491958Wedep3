About Siyazama Civil Construction

Company Overview

Siyazama Civil Construction is a professional construction organization founded in November 2024 by Othamu Ncube and Panashe Zhou. Based in the heart of Port Elizabeth, Eastern Cape, we have quickly established ourselves as trusted building contractors known for excellence and reliability.

Siyazama is renowned for building houses from scratch to finish, with a track record of successful projects that consistently exceed client expectations. Our commitment to leaving every customer with a smile on their face is at the core of everything we do.

We are proud to serve residential and commercial clients across the Eastern Cape province, delivering high-quality construction services that combine traditional craftsmanship with modern building techniques.
